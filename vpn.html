Enterprise-Grade VPN Services

Overview

At HARGAJA-TECH, we design and deploy robust VPN architectures tailored to your organization’s needs. From high-performance site-to-site tunnels that link branch offices securely, to flexible SSL client-based access for remote teams, our solutions ensure end-to-end encryption, zero-trust compliance, and seamless user experience 24/7.

Key Features

  • High-Availability Clustering – Active/active gateways for continuous uptime.
  • Advanced Encryption – AES-256 and perfect forward secrecy for maximum data protection.
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) – Layered user verification via OTP, tokens, or push-based apps.
  • Centralized Policy Management – Single pane of glass for user, device, and network policies.
  • Dynamic Access Control – Role-based and context-aware segmentation for least-privilege access.
  • Cloud & Hybrid Integration – Secure tunnels to public cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and on-prem data centers.

Use Case: Branch Office Connectivity

A multinational enterprise deployed our IPSec site-to-site VPN to interconnect 20+ branch offices across Europe. By integrating redundant VPN concentrators and dynamic routing, they achieved sub-second failover, consistent bandwidth, and end-to-end encryption for all interoffice traffic.

Use Case: Secure Remote Workforce

For a global services firm with 5,000+ mobile employees, we rolled out SSL VPN portals and a lightweight client. Users connect from any device—laptop, tablet, or smartphone—and gain secure access to corporate apps, file shares, and intranet resources without compromising performance or security posture.
