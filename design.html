Strategic Network Architecture

Overview

HARGAJA-TECH delivers end-to-end network design services—from initial requirements gathering and capacity planning to detailed topology diagrams and technology selection. We ensure your network meets performance, scalability, and resilience goals while aligning with business objectives and industry best practices.

Key Components

  • Logical & Physical Topology – Design of core, distribution, and access layers.
  • Routing & Switching Architecture – Protocol selection (OSPF, BGP), spine-leaf or collapsed core.
  • High Availability – Redundancy with VRRP/HSRP, link aggregation, and multi-chassis setups.
  • Capacity Planning – Bandwidth forecasting, QoS policy design for voice, video, and data.
  • SD-WAN & Cloud Connectivity – Hybrid WAN strategies, MPLS-to-Internet offload.
  • Wireless & IoT Integration – Site surveys, controller-based WLAN, IoT segmentation.

Use Case: Corporate Campus

For a multi-building corporate campus, we crafted a modular network design with dual-core switches, redundant fiber rings, and PoE access layers. This architecture supports high-throughput data, VoIP services, and secure guest Wi-Fi—all managed via a centralized network controller.

Use Case: Data Center Fabric

A large e-commerce provider required a low-latency data center fabric. We implemented a spine-leaf design using Cisco Nexus platforms, enabled EVPN-VXLAN overlay for multi-tenancy, and automated configuration with Ansible—delivering seamless east-west traffic and zero-touch provisioning.
