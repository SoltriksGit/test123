Cisco Identity Services Engine

Overview

Cisco Identity Services Engine (ISE) is a robust policy management platform that ensures secure network access by dynamically identifying, profiling, and enforcing policies for every device and user. It unifies authentication, authorization, and accounting (AAA) functions across wired, wireless, and VPN environments, providing centralized security control.

Key Features

  • Device Profiling & Posture Assessment – Continuous evaluation of device health and compliance.
  • Guest & BYOD Onboarding – Secure self-service portals for guests and personal devices.
  • Dynamic VLAN & ACL Assignment – Automated network segmentation based on user roles.
  • Integration with Cisco DNA Center & PKI – Centralized orchestration and certificate-based authentication.

Use Case: University Campus

At a large university, Cisco ISE provides role-based access for students, faculty, and guests. By leveraging dynamic profiling and policy enforcement, the campus network ensures only compliant devices connect, simplifies onboarding, and maintains continuous security across all access methods.
