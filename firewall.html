Next-Generation Firewall

Overview

Our firewall solutions provide a multi-layered defense to protect your network perimeter, data center, and cloud environments. We implement stateful packet inspection, deep packet inspection (DPI), and behavior-based threat detection to block attacks before they compromise your infrastructure.

Key Features

  • Intrusion Prevention System (IPS) – Real-time detection and blocking of exploits and malware.
  • Application Control – Granular policies for over 4,000 applications.
  • Threat Intelligence – Automated updates from global threat feeds.
  • User & Device Identity – Role-based policies tied to user directories (LDAP, AD).
  • SSL/TLS Inspection – Decrypt and inspect encrypted traffic without performance impact.
  • High-Performance Clustering – Scale out with active/active firewall clusters for zero downtime.

Use Case: Data Center Protection

For a financial services data center, we deployed a clustered next-generation firewall with strict east-west segmentation. By combining IPS, threat intelligence, and user-based policies, the organization saw a 90% reduction in successful lateral attacks within the first month.

Use Case: Cloud & Hybrid Environments

A tech startup with hybrid workloads in AWS and on-premises needed consistent policy enforcement. We integrated virtual firewalls in their VPCs and extended management to on-prem hardware, delivering unified logging, automated rule deployment, and compliance reporting across both environments.
